Learning Management System (LMS)

close

Description

A system that the whole school uses to interact with each other:

- Teacher to create assignments, exams, chat and to take attendance.

- Student to answer exams, view assignments, chat and view his schedules.

- Admin to make announcements and to keep track of everything.

Client(s)

Cardiff Modern School (CMS).

New Capital International School (CMS).

Manaret El-Eman International School (MEIS).

Merryland International School (MIS).

Done by Native (DbN).
